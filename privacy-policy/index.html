Privacy Policy for Office Rumble

Last updated: November 2025

Office Rumble ("the game") is developed by an independent developer. This policy explains what information the game collects and how that information is used.

Information We Collect

The game uses Epic Online Services (EOS) for login and multiplayer features. When you log in using your Epic Games account, EOS may provide the following basic account information:

  • Your display name
  • Your Epic Account ID
  • Your online status
  • Your list of Epic friends (only for in-game friend features)

No additional personal information is collected by the developer.

How Information Is Used

Your information is used only to enable gameplay features such as online login, matchmaking, and multiplayer interactions.

Your data is not sold, shared, or used for advertising.
